Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI search?
Papri uses state-of-the-art AI models trained on vast amounts of every contect data available Online. It analyzes transcripts, visual elements, and context to provide highly relevant results. Accuracy improves continuously as the AI learns, but complex or ambiguous queries might occasionally yield less precise results. We aim for pinpoint accuracy in most common use cases like finding specific spoken phrases or visually distinct objects/scenes.
What video formats and sources are supported?
Currently, Papri supports direct links from major platforms like YouTube, Instagram, Tiktok, and Vimeo. You can also upload common file formats (MP4, MOV, AVI, etc. - subject to reasonable size limits on the free tier). We are continuously working to expand support for more platforms and formats based on user feedback.
Is my uploaded content secure?
Yes, security is a top priority. Uploaded content are processed securely, and we employ industry-standard practices for data protection and privacy. Your content is used solely for the purpose of providing the search results you requested. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for detailed information.
How does the 3 free searches limit work?
To let you experience Papri's capabilities, we offer 3 free searches. This count is tracked using a browser cookie. Each time you submit a search (either via URL or upload), the counter decreases. Once you reach zero, the search interface will be disabled, prompting you to subscribe for unlimited access. This limit persists even if you refresh the page or close your browser.
What happens after I pay? How do I get the signup code?
Upon successful payment verification via Stripe, a unique 6-digit signup code will be instantly displayed on the screen within the payment confirmation message. We will ALSO send this code to the email address you provided during checkout. You'll need this code only for your *first* signup to create your Papri account.
Can I trim or download video segments?
The ability to trim and download the specific video segments identified by Papri is a planned feature for our Pro plan subscribers and is currently under development ("coming soon"). Our goal is to allow you to easily extract the exact moments you find without needing external editing software for simple clips.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, the $6 plan is billed monthly, and you can cancel anytime before your next billing date through your account settings (once logged in) to avoid future charges.
